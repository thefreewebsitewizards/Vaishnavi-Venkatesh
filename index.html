About Me

Hi, I'm Vaishnavi Venkatesh — a lifestyle, fashion, and home content creator based in Michigan. As a proud mom to my son Arinjay and a passionate storyteller, I love blending family life, creativity, and style into everything I share.

My content ranges from fashion finds, home décor, beauty, and family adventures to brand collaborations across Amazon, Shein, Halara, Temu, Onemix, Tineco, AOSU, and many more. I'm all about real-life storytelling—whether that's trying out the latest tech, styling a sari, upgrading my home, or sharing mom-life hacks that actually make life easier.

I've built a strong, engaged community across platforms—200k+ on Instagram, 30k+ on TikTok, and a growing Amazon storefront audience (68% US-based). My audience comes to me for authentic reviews, relatable lifestyle tips, and creative brand content.

As a creator, I combine visual storytelling, trend-savvy content, and a family-first approach to deliver impactful campaigns. My work has included UGC videos, Amazon reviews, TikTok trends, Instagram reels, and custom brand storytelling with a personal, engaging touch.

When I'm not creating, you'll find me exploring new Michigan eateries, planning family trips, renovating my home, or experimenting with AI tools to push creative boundaries.
